### Best Value for Money
- **Player Zone (PZTA)**, Serbia — transparent pricing from €1,100/mo, full-service
- **Ilirija Tennis Academy**, Croatia — from €630/week including tourism experiences
- **HTA Academy**, Slovakia — individual lessons from €37/hr
- **Vicio & Fede**, Poland — ~€32/hr private lessons

### Best for Elite/Professional Development
- **TK Agrofert Prostějov**, Czech Republic — produced more Grand Slam champions than any other East European academy
- **Tipsarević Academy**, Serbia — full-boarding, headed by former World No. 8
- **Ljubičić Academy**, Croatia — headed by former World No. 3 & Federer's coach
- **Love4Tennis**, Slovakia — 60+ pro players, Rybakina (Wimbledon champion) as alumna
